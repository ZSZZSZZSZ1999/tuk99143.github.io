About Me

My name is shenze zhang and I am a composition student from china and I focus on chamber and orchestral music.I study piano with prof liu from Jilin College of The Arts since i was 5 years old and started to learn composition when i was 15 years old, and studied harmony and composition with Dr qiao of Jilin College of The Arts and composition with Dr xiu of The Central Conservatory of Music in beijin. my main work is piano trio “ in the faraway land” , quartet no 1, symphony no 1 ,and a series of chamber music using Chinese folk song theme. His primary mentors include Dr Maurice wright , Dr krzywicki in temple.

my works

Repeat On E

This piece was written in fall of 2021 and it’s originated from a joke between me and my friends, they are all fantastic string players and one day they told me why you composers always write very complicate , harsh melody? So that i decided to write a piece with only one pitch at the beginning and then develop it.

Cry in the Wind

This piece was finished in the spring of 2022 and in order to attend SaxandvillaS international composition competition. At that time i was very fascinated with approaching multiphonics on wind instrument and i think that technique can create a timbre, a sound which is very malleable and astonishing, and then this piece was created and has been reading by some of my friends. The multiphonics on wind blend very well with the piano

Mediation At Midnight

Meditation at Midnight was composed last summer and represents the time when I try to quiet down and clear my mind at night before I start to work. The whole piece is basically divided into three stages: trying to get into meditation, being influenced by thought, and finally finding peace. All the small elements in this piece are in flux.
